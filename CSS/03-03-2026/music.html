Music Production Division

Music Producer

About the Position:

The Music Producer will oversee the creative and technical development of songs from concept to final release. This role involves composing, arranging, supervising recording sessions, and collaborating with artists to craft high-quality music productions aligned with the company’s artistic vision.

Bond:

1–2 year creative contract commitment.

Required Qualifications:
  1. Strong knowledge of music composition and arrangement
  2. Proficiency in DAWs (Logic Pro, Ableton, FL Studio, etc.)
  3. Understanding of current global music trends

Experience:

Minimum 2 years of music production experience or a strong production portfolio required.

Salary:

₹80,000 – ₹1,50,000 per month (Performance-based incentives applicable)

Benefits:
  1. Access to professional studio facilities
  2. Creative collaboration with top artists
  3. Royalty and publishing opportunities
  4. Global exposure and industry networking

Sound Engineer

About the Position:

The Sound Engineer is responsible for recording, editing, and enhancing audio quality during studio sessions and live performances. The role requires technical expertise in sound equipment, microphones, and mixing consoles to ensure high-quality output.

Bond:

1-year employment agreement.

Required Qualifications:
  1. Diploma/Degree in Sound Engineering or Audio Technology
  2. Strong understanding of recording equipment and acoustics
  3. Knowledge of mixing consoles and audio software

Experience:

Minimum 1 year of studio or live sound experience preferred.

Salary:

₹50,000 – ₹90,000 per month

Benefits:
  1. Work in professional-grade studios
  2. Exposure to high-profile music projects
  3. Technical skill enhancement programs
  4. Health and wellness benefits

Mixing & Mastering Specialist

About the Position:

The Mixing & Mastering Specialist will refine recorded tracks to achieve balanced, polished, and release-ready audio. This role ensures optimal sound quality across streaming platforms, broadcast, and live events.

Bond:

1-year technical contract.

Required Qualifications:
  1. Advanced knowledge of mixing and mastering techniques
  2. Expertise in plugins, EQ, compression, and sound design
  3. Strong attention to audio detail

Experience:

Minimum 2 years of professional mixing/mastering experience required.

Salary:

₹70,000 – ₹1,20,000 per month

Benefits:
  1. Access to premium audio software and tools
  2. Credit recognition on released tracks
  3. Performance-based bonuses
  4. International project exposure

Recording Studio Assistant

About the Position:

The Recording Studio Assistant supports producers and engineers during recording sessions. Responsibilities include equipment setup, session documentation, track organization, and maintaining studio efficiency.

Bond:

6-month service agreement.

Required Qualifications:
  1. Basic knowledge of audio equipment and DAWs
  2. Strong organizational skills
  3. Passion for music production

Experience:

Freshers may apply; internship experience preferred.

Salary / Stipend:

₹20,000 – ₹30,000 per month

Benefits:
  1. Hands-on studio experience
  2. Direct mentorship from senior engineers
  3. Skill development workshops
  4. Opportunity for full-time placement
