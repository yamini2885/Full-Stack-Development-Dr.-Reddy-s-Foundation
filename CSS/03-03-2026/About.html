Founded in 2005 by visionary producer Bang Si-hyuk, HYBE Corporation began as Big Hit Entertainment with a bold ambition — to create music that resonates beyond borders. Built on a foundation of artistic integrity and long-term artist development, the company focused on authentic storytelling and meaningful connections between artists and fans. From its earliest days, HYBE believed that music could inspire, heal, and unite people around the world.

The company’s breakthrough came with the global rise of BTS, whose unprecedented international success reshaped the global perception of Korean music. Their impact demonstrated HYBE’s commitment to nurturing talent with creative freedom and strategic vision. This milestone marked a turning point, transforming the company from a small entertainment label into a leading global force in the music industry.

In 2021, Big Hit Entertainment rebranded as HYBE, signaling a new era of innovation and expansion. The company broadened its portfolio beyond artist management into technology, digital platforms, and immersive fan experiences. Through initiatives like the fan platform Weverse, HYBE pioneered new ways for audiences to engage with content and artists in a rapidly evolving digital world.

Today, HYBE stands at the intersection of entertainment and technology, continuing to push boundaries across music, content production, and global partnerships. With a vision to build an integrated entertainment ecosystem, HYBE remains committed to creativity, innovation, and delivering experiences that connect people everywhere.
