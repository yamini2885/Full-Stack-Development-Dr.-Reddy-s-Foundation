Business & Strategy

Marketing Strategist

About the Position:

The Marketing Strategist will develop and execute innovative marketing campaigns to promote artists, music releases, and digital platforms. This role focuses on brand positioning, audience growth, and data-driven campaign optimization across global markets.

Bond:

1-year employment agreement.

Required Qualifications:
  1. Degree in Marketing, Business, or related field
  2. Strong understanding of digital marketing and social media strategies
  3. Knowledge of analytics tools and campaign performance tracking

Experience:

Minimum 2 years of experience in marketing or brand strategy preferred.

Salary:

₹70,000 – ₹1,30,000 per month

Benefits:
  1. Work on global brand campaigns
  2. Performance-based incentives
  3. Professional growth opportunities
  4. Health and wellness benefits

  2. Strong organizational and communication skills
  3. Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously

Experience:

Minimum 1–2 years experience in talent management or entertainment coordination preferred.

Salary:

₹60,000 – ₹1,10,000 per month

Benefits:
