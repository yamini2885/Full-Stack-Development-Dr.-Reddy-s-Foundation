Artist Division

Vocal Artist Trainee

About the Position:

The Vocal Artist Trainee program is designed to develop emerging talent through structured vocal training, performance workshops, and studio exposure. Trainees will work closely with professional vocal coaches and performance directors to refine their artistic identity.

Bond:

1-year training commitment agreement.

Required Qualifications:

  1. Strong vocal foundation (classical or contemporary)
  2. Basic knowledge of music theory preferred
  3. Passion for stage performance

Experience:

Freshers may apply; prior stage/singing competition experience is an advantage.

Salary / Stipend:

₹25,000 - ₹40,000 per month (Training Stipend)

Benefits:

  1. Professional vocal coaching
  2. Studio recording opportunities
  3. Performance showcases
  4. Career mentorship

Professional Dancer / Performer

About the Position:

Responsible for delivering high-energy stage performances, music video choreography, and live event appearances. Candidates will train in multiple dance styles including hip-hop, contemporary, and K-pop performance styles.

Bond:

1-2 year performance contract.

Required Qualifications:

  1. Formal dance training
  2. Strong rhythm and flexibility
  3. Ability to learn choreography quickly

Experience:

Minimum 1 year performance or competition experience preferred.

Salary / Stipend:

₹40,000 – ₹80,000 per month (based on skill & role)

Benefits:

  1. Professional choreography training
  2. Performance tours
  3. Fitness & wellness support
  4. Brand collaboration opportunities
